    2-Minute Maggi Recipe

    This is my favorite hostel recipe. It's quick, easy, and super tasty for late-night cravings!

    Ingredients

    • 1 Packet Maggi Noodles
    • 1 and 1/2 Cups Water
    • 1 Maggi Tastemaker
    • 1 Chopped Onion (optional)
    • 1 Chopped Tomato (optional)

    Instructions

    1. Boil 1 and 1/2 cups of water in a pan.
    2. Add Maggi noodles and tastemaker to the boiling water.
    3. Cook for 2 minutes and stir occasionally.
    4. Add chopped veggies if you want and mix well.
    5. Serve hot and enjoy your Maggi!
